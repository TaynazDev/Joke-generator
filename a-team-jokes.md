# Family-Friendly A-Team Jokes

## 1. The Plan
**Q:** Why did Hannibal always smile?  
**A:** Because he loved it when a plan comes together... even if it's just his grocery list!

## 2. Mr. T's Breakfast
**Q:** What does Mr. T eat for breakfast?  
**A:** He doesn't eat breakfast—breakfast pities the fool who tries to wake him up early!

## 3. B.A.'s Fear
**Q:** Why doesn't B.A. Baracus like to fly?  
**A:** Because even tough guys are allowed to have their ups and downs... mostly downs!

## 4. Face's Disguise
**Q:** Why was Face so good at disguises?  
**A:** Because he always put his best face forward!

## 5. The Van
**Q:** What's black, red, and can't stop moving?  
**A:** The A-Team van—it's got places to go and bad guys to catch!

## 6. Hannibal's Disguises
**Q:** Why did Hannibal wear so many disguises?  
**A:** He wanted to keep his identity under wraps!

## 7. Mr. T's Jewelry
**Q:** How much does Mr. T's gold jewelry weigh?  
**A:** A ton of pity for anyone who tries to steal it!

## 8. Flying Problem
**Q:** What's the A-Team's least favorite type of mission?  
**A:** Anything that requires B.A. to get on a plane—they have to plan around his plan to avoid the plan!

## 9. Team Building
**Q:** Why is the A-Team so good at construction?  
**A:** Because they can build a tank out of spare parts in 20 minutes!

## 10. Milk Mistake
**Q:** Why did Mr. T refuse to drink the milk?  
**A:** He only drinks his milk when HE says so—he pities the cow who disagrees!

## 11. Hannibal's Smoke
**Q:** Why does Hannibal always have a cigar?  
**A:** So he can say "I love it when a plan comes together" with extra drama!

## 12. Murdock's Flying
**Q:** Why is Murdock the best pilot?  
**A:** Because he's always flying high—even when he's on the ground!

## 13. The Escape
**Q:** How does the A-Team always escape?  
**A:** They've got a van with a plan and a team that never says "can't"!

## 14. Face's Charm
**Q:** Why did Face always get them out of trouble?  
**A:** Because he knew how to face the facts and charm his way through!

## 15. Workshop Wonders
**Q:** What's B.A.'s favorite room?  
**A:** The workshop—where he can wrench away his problems!

## 16. Military Police
**Q:** Why can't the Military Police ever catch the A-Team?  
**A:** Because the A-Team is always one step ahead and two disguises better!

## 17. Murdock's Hospital
**Q:** Why does Murdock stay at the VA hospital?  
**A:** Free room service and the perfect alibi—plus the pudding cups!

## 18. Team Transport
**Q:** What's faster than a speeding bullet and painted red, black, and grey?  
**A:** The A-Team van when they're being chased by the MPs!

## 19. Golden Rule
**Q:** What's Mr. T's golden rule?  
**A:** Treat others with respect... or prepare to be pitied!

## 20. Mission Success
**Q:** Why does every A-Team mission succeed?  
**A:** Because with Hannibal's plans, Face's charm, B.A.'s muscles, and Murdock's flying—they're an A+ team!
